**Duration**: 2025 -- Present

**Role**: Lead researcher of work package 4: smart data for data analysis and predictive modeling.

Collaborated with Menzis (Dutch health insurance company) and Prof. dr. Claudine Lamoth (Professor of Movement Analysis & Smart Technology in Aging, Dept. of Human Movement Sciences, UMCG) on the SamenGezond dataset---one of the world's largest multimodal health monitoring datasets, spanning 10+ years with 100,000+ participants. Data includes demographics, wearable sensor recordings (physical activity, sleep, heart rate), insurance records, and platform interaction logs.
