**Duration**: 2022 -- 2023

Partnered with a Dutch professional sports club to collect multi-year longitudinal data on training loads, performance, and injuries. Built injury risk prediction models using machine learning and survival analysis, quantifying the impact of training intensity and recovery periods. Provided scientific recommendations for coaching teams to prevent injuries and extend athletes' careers.
